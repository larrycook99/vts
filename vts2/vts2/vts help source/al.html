BACnet Application Services

In its primary capacity as a BACnet client, VTS can be used to test the response from a BACnet device or group of devices to any of the BACnet application layer confirmed or unconfirmed service requests. You accomplish this task using the VTS AL menu. The correspondence between the BACnet services and the VTS menu is shown in the tables below. The tables also serve as hyperlinks to help for the specified service requests.
